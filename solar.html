Overview

The Dual Axis Solar Tracking System is designed to enhance the efficiency of solar panels by continuously aligning them with the sun's position throughout the day. Unlike fixed systems, this setup allows movement along both horizontal and vertical axes, ensuring maximum sunlight exposure from sunrise to sunset. The system uses Light Dependent Resistors (LDRs) to detect the sun's direction and servo motors to adjust the panel orientation accordingly. By optimizing the angle of incidence, the system significantly increases energy capture, making it ideal for sustainable energy applications.

Core Components

  • ESP8266
  • LDR Sensors
  • Servo Motors
  • Solar Panel

Key Features

  • Tracks sunlight in both horizontal and vertical directions.
  • Maximizes solar panel energy efficiency by ~30%
  • Automatically adjusts using LDR sensors.

Outcome

Achieved up to 30% higher energy output compared to static panels through real-time sun tracking. The project was successfully showcased at the IEEE PELS 2024 exhibition and secured a place in the top five.
